Step by step guidence about how our website works.




Step 1: Register with a Username, Email id and password.







Step 2: Go back to home screen after a successful registration.







Step 3: Login using registered email id and password. If forgot, "forgot password" is there for your help.







Step 4: Enter your marks in percentage (%) and choose subject to find suitable college for you.







Step 5: List of best matched colleges are there or error if not qualified to fullfill the required critaria.







Step 6: You can choose a college to save it in your profile.







Step 7: Check your profile to get the details of your saved college.
